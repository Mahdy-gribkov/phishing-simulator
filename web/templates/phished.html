What went wrong?
You clicked a link in an email that appeared to come from a trusted source. A real attacker could have stolen your credentials, installed malware, or compromised your accounts.

How to protect yourself:
• Always check the sender's actual email address (not just the display name)
• Hover over links before clicking — check the domain
• Be suspicious of urgency and authority tactics
• When in doubt, go directly to the website instead of clicking email links
